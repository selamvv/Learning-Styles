Learning Styles

Carol Dweck argues that mindsets is the biggest setback for students trying to complete their education. There is the fixed mindset, which is when one attempt to always know what they are doing and run away from anything that they don’t know how to do. These students also believe that trying is a negative thing, they don’t believe people work to get intelligent, they think you are born smart or you are not and lastly they understand that mistakes are a part of learning.
